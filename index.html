SehatSutra

Note: This is not a working product or a final product, this is only a dispaly/prototype of how the basic features of our idea once we have completed development. This will not respond to any prompts other than the ones we will use for showcasing our idea.

Our system enables users to textually input or capture images of their meals, which are analyzed using a deep learning model trained specifical ly on Indian dishes and thalis. Each meal is mapped to nutrient data from the Indian Food Composition Tables, providing accurate estimates of calories and nutrient intake. Beyond daily tracking, SehatSutra uses predictive modeling to forecast future deficiencies and recommends balanced, region and season aware foods suited to dietary preferences (vegetarian, vegan, non-vegetarian).

An AI nutrition coach interacts conversationally, encouraging better habits using positive reinforcement and culturally relevant guidance. By combining computer vision, predictive analytics, reasoning AI, and behavioral modeling, SehatSutra goes beyond tracking, it understands, predicts, and guides in ways current AI systems cannot. The project aims to make nutrition awareness intelligent, empathetic, and deeply rooted in India's cultural food diversity, yet led by technology and Artificial Intelligence.
